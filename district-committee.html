The Northern district covers the regions from the border of North Yorkshire northward through Cleveland, County Durham, Tyne & Wear and Northumberland. There are 27 Spiritualist Churches/Centres with around 1,500 members in our district and over 120 individual Spiritualists who are members of the SNU.
